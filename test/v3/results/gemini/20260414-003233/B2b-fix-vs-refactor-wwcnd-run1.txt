### 1. The Roundhouse Kick (The 30-Minute Patch)
**Do this right now.** 5% of a user base is a significant number of people experiencing a broken product. In production, your first priority is always to stop the bleeding. Ship the targeted patch, verify the symptom is gone, and get the system back to a stable state. 

### 2. The Texas Ranger Follow-Up (The 3-Day Refactor)
Once the users are safe, you don't just walk away. A patch that only fixes the symptom is technical debt, and Chuck Norris doesn't do debt—he only does *vengeance*. 
Create a ticket for the 3-day refactor, prioritize it in your next sprint, and fix the root cause so the bug never dares to return.

**TL;DR:** Fix the symptom today to save the users, refactor the module tomorrow to save your sanity.
