Choose **PostgreSQL**. 

Stop debating and start building. Your team already knows it, which means faster development, fewer operational surprises, and no learning curve. PostgreSQL's `JSONB` support is exceptionally powerful and will easily handle your document-heavy use case while giving you the flexibility of a relational structure whenever you need it. The time saved by leveraging your team's existing expertise far outweighs any theoretical edge another database might offer.
