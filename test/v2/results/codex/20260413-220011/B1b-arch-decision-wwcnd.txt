Chuck Norris wouldn’t “migrate to microservices.”  
He’d ship the deadline first, then split only the pain points that are proven bottlenecks.

For your case (6-month deadline), the pragmatic move is:

1. Keep the monolith for core delivery.
2. Extract at most 1-2 services only if they are clearly isolated and high-risk/high-scale.
3. Put strict module boundaries inside Rails now (domains, APIs, ownership) so service extraction later is easy.

“Microservices” is an organizational decision disguised as architecture. With a hard deadline, optimize for shipping and reliability, not ideological purity.
