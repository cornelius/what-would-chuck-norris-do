Usually it points to a playful mindset, but with useful subtext:

- **Concepts:** toughness, self-reliance, courage, decisiveness, discipline.
- **Attitudes:** confidence under pressure, no-excuses accountability, persistence, calm aggression toward obstacles.
- **Problem-solving style:** simplify first, act quickly, favor practical over theoretical, take ownership, push through friction, and adapt without overthinking.

In short, it’s often shorthand for: **“Be bold, stay resilient, and handle it.”**
